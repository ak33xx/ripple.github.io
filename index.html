Our Mission

The Ripple Effect is based on the understanding that we are all connected. Thoughts and actions create ripples that travel outward causing an effect in our environment. The digital era is changing the way we live, work, and relate to one another. Every action we take or the thought we express, affects the people in our circle and their reactions in turn affect others.

All of us have an ability and capacity to impact the world in small ways for better or worse. At RIPPLE, we hope to enable individuals from all parts of the world to participate in interests, benefit from diverse view-points, and reflect. So share your thoughts and let them flow. We’ll make them Ripple through space and time.











































What is RIPPLE

Ripple and “Rippling” is about broadcasting daily thoughts, views, opinion to the world, with the hope that your messages are useful and interesting to someone. In other words, microblogging. Conversely, Ripple is also about discovering interesting people online and following their thoughts in form of messages. You may forward or share someone’s ‘Ripple’ that you find interesting as a Ri-pple. Every microblog 'Ripple' entry is limited to 150 characters or less .

How does RIPPLE work

Ripple is very simple to use as broadcaster or receiver. You join with a free account and Ripple name. Then you send broadcasts daily, or even hourly. Go to the 'Write a post' box, type 150 characters or less, and click 'Ripple'. You may include photo, video or a hyperlink. To receive Ripple feeds, you simply find someone interesting (celebrities included), and 'follow' them to subscribe to their Ripple microblogs.
